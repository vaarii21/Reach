Welcome to R.E.A.C.H

Responsibility to Engineer Care and Humanity


We are a fast growing charity platform in India dedicated to make the Rural and tribal lives
in the corners and depths of the country better. We aim to be their torch in hand as they come into the light of this world.



"Be Among Them"

"...We need more and more organisations like R.E.A.C.H and more and more youngsters joining them to make our country a better place. I wish you the very best"

- Narendra Modi, Prime Minister of India.



VISION

To uplift the rural and minority communities in India
to higher social status and eradicate all the discrimination,
poverty and communal difficulties they face.

MISSION

To ensure better education, food, sanitation and other resources
for those in need by which they will find the strength
to stand on their feet again.
